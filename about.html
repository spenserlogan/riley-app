About Me

Heyo! I’m Riley. I love period-piece dramas on PBS, chai tea lattes and EARRINGS—you’ll almost never see me without a pair on! I’ve never met a Christmas tree I didn’t like, and my dog, Adelaide, is arguably a foxier redhead than I am! I decided to dive headfirst into adventure elopements because they spoke to me on a very personal level. I did the whole big white wedding thing, and while it was lovely, and there’s no right or wrong way to get married, as someone who doesn’t necessarily like being the center of attention, and craves exploring with my bae all day every day, elopements check all the boxes. Traveling is my biggest passion, with loving relationships being a close second. Marrying the two to help couples create a perfectly intimate, tailored to you, badass wedding day is what I’m aiming for! I truly believe love and adventure go hand-in-hand; wedded together. I have years of experience exploring some of the most beautiful places on Earth and a long list of ideas for you and your honey to kick off your biggest adventure yet—marriage! All you need to know is that you’re ready to pledge your love to one another, and I can help with the rest!
