How to Elope!

  1. Fall madly in love with your honey and get engaged!
  2. Inquire! Send a message my way so we can get this show on the road!
  3. Let’s chat about your big day! Tell me your vision, hopes, and dreams for your elopement! If you don’t have every detail planned out, that’s perfectly fine. I’m here to help you fill in the blanks!
  4. Now for a little formalization. Sign an agreement and make a deposit! You’re one step closer to the best day ever!
  5. Dot the I’s and cross the T’s. This is the fun part! This is where we nail down accommodations, activities, and delightful details like popping champagne, your marriage license, sweet treats, etc!
  6. GET MARRIED! Vow to love your sweetie from now until forever, and enjoy every waking moment stress free! I’ll take care of the rest— photos, finding the best spots, your timeline and all that jazz!
